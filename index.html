About Me

Hello! I'm Yuvraj Sapkota, a 22-year-old from Nepal. As i was born in India, My academic journey began in India, where I completed my Class 10 at the esteemed St. Joseph's High School. The experience of studying in India enriched my perspective and laid a solid foundation for my future .

Subsequently, I pursued my higher education in Nepal, where I completed my Plus Two in Computer Science at Oxford College. It was during this time that I delved into the world of computer science, and my passion for technology and programming blossomed.

Continuing my educational path at Oxford College, I joined the Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) course. Here, I've had the opportunity to deepen my knowledge of computer science and web development, which has become a significant part of my life. The dynamic field of web development has allowed me to express my creativity and problem-solving skills, and I'm dedicated to perfecting my craft.

In addition to my formal education, I'm also pursuing a keen interest in graphic design. I believe that combining web development and graphic design can lead to the creation of stunning, user-centric websites that leave a lasting impression.

My journey has been a fusion of diverse experiences, cultures, and educational environments, and I'm excited to continue my growth as a student, a web developer, and a graphic designer. I'm always eager to collaborate, learn, and share knowledge with those who share my passion for technology and design. Let's explore the digital world together!
